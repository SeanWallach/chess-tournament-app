Welcome to the West End Chess Crew Tournament site!

The Rules:

- 5-6 rounds, 10 minutes + 5 sec increment
- Swiss style tournament (no one gets eliminated)
- you get matched with people who have done just as well or as poorly as yourself
- points: 1 for win, 0.5 for draw, 0 for loss
- no one person should get the same color more than twice in a row
- there is a tiebreak for 1st place with 5 minute time control
